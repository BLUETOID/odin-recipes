Cold Coffee Recipe

Description

This cold coffee recipe is perfect for hot summer days when you need a refreshing and energizing drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew a cup of strong coffee and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. In a blender, combine the cooled coffee, milk, sugar, and ice cubes.
  3. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Pour the cold coffee into a tall glass.
  5. Top with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
